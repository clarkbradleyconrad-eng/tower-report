WR · Jr.
Cam Coleman
From: Auburn · On3: 99.1 · #7 WR nationally
The marquee portal addition of the 2026 cycle. Coleman was a 5-star recruit who never got the ball enough at Auburn. In Texas's air-raid system with Arch Manning, he projects as a true WR1. Elite separation, contested catch ability, and big-play speed. This addition alone upgrades the offense by a tier.
Projection: 70+ catches, 1,100+ yards, 10 TDs · WR1
RB · Sr.
Raleek Brown
From: Oklahoma State · 1,300 rush yds in 2025
The most experienced and explosive back in the Big 12, now in burnt orange. Brown brings home run ability that Texas lacked in recent years. Exceptional in the open field, capable receiver out of the backfield. Immediate starter who gives Sarkisian a true featured back option.
Projection: 180+ carries, 1,100+ yards, 8 TD · RB1
CB · Jr.
Derek Williams Jr.
From: Penn State · 2025 Big Ten All-Conference
Texas desperately needed a true CB1 after losing coverage to graduation. Williams brings elite man-coverage skills and the physicality the SEC demands. His arrival changes the entire defensive ceiling. With Colin Simmons as pass rush and Williams on the back end, this defense could be elite.
Projection: CB1 starter · 8+ PBUs · 3+ INTs
