X Posting Settings
Posting Mode
Controls how posts move from generated to posted.
Manual Approval
Every post sits in the queue until you explicitly approve it. Nothing goes out without a human click. Best for launch or sensitive periods.
Trusted Sources Auto-Approve
Posts sourced from your trusted sources list (247Sports, On3, etc.) are auto-approved and scheduled. All others still require manual review.
Fully Automatic
All generated posts are auto-approved and scheduled within your daily limits and minimum interval. Use with caution — no human review step.
Max Posts Per Day
Hard cap on posts in a 24h period. Approved posts beyond this limit will queue and post the next day.
5
Min Interval Between Posts
Minimum minutes between consecutive posts. Prevents flooding the feed.

Link Ratio
Target percentage of posts that include a link to a Tower Report story. 0% = text-only, 50% = half linked.
