2027 Class Overview — June 3, 2026
Tower Analysis
Texas enters the summer visit season with eleven commitments in the 2027 class and a trajectory that projects into a top-10 national finish if the staff closes on its remaining priority targets. The class is built around a blue-chip anchor at wide receiver — Easton Royal checks in above 97 on On3's composite — and a defensive front that gains real depth with Cameron Hall and Derwin Fields already signed on. The offensive line remains the most critical open position: Jackson Cook gives Texas a credentialed OL early, but the staff needs at least one more blue-chip prospect before early signing period to fully close that gap. At quarterback, the 2027 need is genuine and the staff knows it — a name to watch closely will emerge this summer. With official visit season opening September 1 and the summer camp circuit underway, the next six weeks are the most important stretch of this cycle. Tower will update signals as news breaks.
